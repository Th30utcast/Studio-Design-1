GDPR Compliance

At HomeQuest, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What data do we collect?

  • Your name, email address, and contact details
  • Login information and account preferences
  • Property listings, messages, and interactions

How do we use your data?

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Communicate important updates
  • Ensure platform security and compliance

Your rights under GDPR

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we store about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and data.
  • Restriction: You can restrict processing of your personal data.
  • Portability: You can request to transfer your data to another provider.
